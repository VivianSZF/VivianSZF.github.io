Zifan Shi

Zifan Shi is a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Dit-Yan Yeung and Prof. Qifeng Chen. Before that, she obtained a Bachelor's degree at Nanjing University in 2019. Her research interests include generative models and 3D vision.

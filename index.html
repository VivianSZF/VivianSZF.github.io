Zifan Shi

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Dit-Yan Yeung and Prof. Qifeng Chen. Before that, I obtained a Bachelor's degree at Nanjing University in 2019. Currently, I am a visiting student researcher at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. I also have a great time working closely with Dr. Yujun Shen and Dr. Yinghao Xu. My research interests include generative models, graphics and 3D vision.
